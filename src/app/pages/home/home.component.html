About Kerala

Kerala is a beautiful state located in the southernmost tip of India with the Arabian sea in the west and the Western Ghats in the east. The state consists of 14 districts.Thiruvananthapuram is the state capital. Lying in the tropical region, the state always experience humid tropical wet climate. The word 'kera' means coconut. Kerala is blessed with abundant number of coconut palms. Kerala’s treasure lies in its mountains,hills,rivers,backwaters,beaches,lakes etc.All these makes Kerala the most beautiful state in India.
